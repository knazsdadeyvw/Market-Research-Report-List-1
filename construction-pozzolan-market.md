<p><p>The construction pozzolan market comprises two main types: artificial and natural pozzolans. Artificial pozzolans are industrial by-products, such as fly ash or slag, created through manufacturing processes and used to enhance concrete properties. In contrast, natural pozzolans are naturally occurring materials, like volcanic ash or zeolite, that possess pozzolanic properties. Both types contribute to improved durability, workability, and sustainability in construction, making them essential for eco-friendly building practices and reducing the carbon footprint of cement production.</p></p>
