<p><p>The biomass charcoal market comprises two main types: bulk biomass briquettes and biomass pellets. Bulk biomass briquettes are compact, cylindrical blocks made from compressed organic material, suitable for heating and cooking due to their efficient burning properties. Biomass pellets, smaller and uniform in size, are typically made from sawdust and other wood residues, offering high energy density and ease of storage and transport. Both types serve as renewable energy sources, promoting sustainability and reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
