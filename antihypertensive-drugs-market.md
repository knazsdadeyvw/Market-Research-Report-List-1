<p><p>The antihypertensive drugs market comprises several key types. Diuretic antihypertensives reduce fluid volume, lowering blood pressure. Sympathetic suppressants inhibit the nervous system's role in blood pressure regulation. Renin-angiotensin system inhibitors block hormones that elevate blood pressure. Calcium antagonists relax blood vessel muscles by inhibiting calcium influx, while vasodilators directly widen blood vessels. Each category offers distinct mechanisms for managing hypertension, catering to diverse patient needs and contributing to the overall growth of the antihypertensive market.</p></p>
