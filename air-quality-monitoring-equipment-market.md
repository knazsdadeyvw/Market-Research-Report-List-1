<p><p>The air quality monitoring equipment market comprises various types, each serving specific functions. Air pollution samplers collect air samples for analysis, while anemometers measure wind speed and direction, crucial for understanding pollutant dispersion. Gas analyzers and detectors identify specific gases in the atmosphere, enhancing pollution control measures. Particle counters assess particulate matter in the air, vital for health risk evaluation. Nitrogen oxides (NOx) monitoring ensures compliance with emission standards, and electrostatic precipitators (ESP) control systems reduce airborne particulate emissions, improving overall air quality.</p></p>
