<p><p>The Agriculture Solar Pumps market comprises several types suited for various irrigation needs. DC Surface Suction pumps are used for shallow water sources, providing efficient surface water access. AC Submersible pumps are ideal for deeper water sources, utilizing alternating current for robust performance. DC Submersible pumps are energy-efficient and designed for deep well applications, while AC Floating pumps operate on the water's surface, suitable for reservoirs. Other types may include hybrid or specially designed pumps catering to unique agricultural requirements.</p></p>
