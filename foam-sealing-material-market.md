<p><p>The foam sealing material market comprises various types, including PU foam, PE foam, and EPDM foam, each serving distinct applications. PU (polyurethane) foam offers excellent insulation and cushioning properties, making it ideal for sealing in construction and automotive sectors. PE (polyethylene) foam is lightweight and moisture-resistant, suitable for packaging and insulation. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) foam is highly durable and weather-resistant, often used in roofing and automotive seals. These materials enhance energy efficiency and provide reliable protection against environmental factors.</p></p>
