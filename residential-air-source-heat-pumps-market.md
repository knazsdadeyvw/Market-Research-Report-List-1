<p><strong>The Residential Air Source Heat Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-Air</li><li>Air-to-Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential air source heat pumps are classified into two main types: air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat from the outside air to indoor air, effectively providing heating in winter and cooling in summer. In contrast, air-to-water heat pumps extract heat from the air to warm water for radiators or underfloor heating, making them suitable for homes with hydronic heating systems. Both options are energy-efficient, reducing reliance on traditional heating sources while maintaining home comfort.</p></p>
