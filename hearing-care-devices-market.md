<p><ul><li>Behind-the-Ear (BTE) Aids</li><li>In-the-Ear Aids</li><li>Receiver-in-the-Ear (RITE)</li><li>Canal Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hearing care devices market comprises several types, including Behind-the-Ear (BTE) aids, which sit behind the ear and are suitable for various hearing losses. In-the-Ear (ITE) aids fit within the outer ear and are custom-made for comfort. Receiver-in-the-Ear (RITE) devices have the speaker positioned in the ear canal, offering a discreet design with enhanced sound quality. Canal hearing aids, including completely-in-canal (CIC), fit deep within the ear canal, providing a nearly invisible option for users with mild to moderate hearing loss.</p></p>
