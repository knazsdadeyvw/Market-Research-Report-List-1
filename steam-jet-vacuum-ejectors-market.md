<p><ul><li>Single Stage Vacuum Ejectors</li><li>Multi-Stage Vacuum Ejectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam jet vacuum ejectors market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage vacuum ejectors. Single-stage vacuum ejectors operate with a single jet of steam to create a vacuum, ideal for applications with lower vacuum requirements. In contrast, multi-stage vacuum ejectors utilize multiple steam jets to achieve deeper vacuum levels, making them suitable for more demanding industrial processes. Both types are widely used in sectors such as chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing for efficient vacuum generation.</p></p>
