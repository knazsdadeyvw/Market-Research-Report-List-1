<p><p>Self-Organising Networks (SON) can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based SON leverage distributed computing resources and scalability, enabling real-time network optimization and management from remote locations, which enhances flexibility and reduces operational costs. On-premise SON, on the other hand, are deployed directly within a network's infrastructure, offering greater control and security for operators. Each type provides unique benefits, allowing telecom providers to select a suitable approach based on their specific operational needs and constraints.</p></p>
