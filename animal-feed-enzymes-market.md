<p><p>The animal feed enzymes market consists of several key types: phytases, carbohydrases, proteases, and others. Phytases enhance phosphorus availability from plant sources, improving nutrient absorption. Carbohydrases break down complex carbohydrates into simpler sugars, aiding digestion and energy utilization. Proteases facilitate protein breakdown, promoting better amino acid availability for growth. Other enzymes may include lipases and xylanases, which support fat digestion and fiber breakdown, respectively. Together, these enzymes optimize livestock nutrition and feed efficiency, driving market growth.</p></p>
