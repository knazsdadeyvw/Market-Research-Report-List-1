<p><p>The solar silicon wafer market primarily consists of two types: single crystal silicon wafers and polycrystalline silicon wafers. Single crystal wafers, made from a single crystal structure, offer higher efficiency and better performance in solar cells, making them the preferred choice for premium applications. In contrast, polycrystalline wafers, composed of multiple crystals, are more cost-effective to produce and offer a decent performance, making them popular for budget-friendly solar solutions. Each type serves distinct customer needs within the solar energy sector.</p></p>
