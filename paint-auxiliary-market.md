<p><p>The Paint Auxiliary Market encompasses various functional additives that enhance paint performance. Rheology modifiers adjust the viscosity and flow properties, ensuring optimal application. Defoamers reduce foam formation during production and application, improving finish quality. Dispersants help maintain a stable distribution of pigments and fillers, preventing settling and clumping. Wetting agents improve the surface wetting of pigments, enhancing adhesion and coating uniformity. Other additives may include biocides and corrosion inhibitors, which enhance durability and longevity of paint formulations.</p></p>
