<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Mutimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optics Testing Device market is divided into two main types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode fibers use a single light path for long-distance communication, providing high bandwidth and low signal attenuation, making them ideal for telecommunication and data centers. Multimode fibers, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distances and are commonly used in local area networks. Each type requires specific testing devices to ensure optimal performance and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
