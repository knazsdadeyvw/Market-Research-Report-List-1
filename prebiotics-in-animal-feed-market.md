<p><p>The prebiotics in the animal feed market include various types that promote gut health and improve digestion. Inulin, derived from chicory roots, enhances beneficial gut bacteria. Fructooligosaccharides (FOS) are short-chain carbohydrates that stimulate the growth of probiotics. Isomaltooligosaccharides (IMO) support digestive health and nutrient absorption. Other prebiotics may include xylooligosaccharides and galactooligosaccharides, each contributing to improved fermentation processes and overall animal health. Collectively, these prebiotics enhance growth performance, nutrient utilization, and immune function in livestock.</p></p>
