<p><strong>The LNG As A Bunker Fuel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Truck To Ship (TTS)</li><li>Port To Ship (PTS)</li><li>Ship To Ship (STS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNG bunker fuel market consists of three main types: Truck to Ship (TTS), where LNG is transported by truck to vessels at the dock for refueling; Port to Ship (PTS), where LNG is supplied from storage facilities at ports directly to ships; and Ship to Ship (STS), which involves transferring LNG from one vessel to another while at sea or in port. These methods provide flexibility for ship operators, offering efficient fuel options to meet environmental regulations.</p></p>
