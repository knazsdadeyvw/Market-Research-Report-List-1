<p><p>The titanium tube market is primarily categorized into cold rolling and welding types. Cold rolling titanium tubes are produced by passively deforming titanium material at room temperature, enhancing strength and surface finish, making them ideal for aerospace and medical applications. Welding titanium tubes, on the other hand, involve joining titanium pieces using welding techniques, providing flexibility in design and assembly. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to specific industrial needs in sectors such as aerospace, automotive, and chemical processing.</p></p>
