<p><p>The audio intercom system market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless audio intercom systems. Wired systems utilize physical cabling for communication, offering reliable connections and typically better sound quality, making them suitable for permanent installations in homes and businesses. In contrast, wireless audio intercom systems operate using radio frequencies or Wi-Fi, providing flexibility and ease of installation without intrusive wiring. This makes them ideal for temporary setups or locations where cabling is impractical.</p></p>
