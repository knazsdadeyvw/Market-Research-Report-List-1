<p><ul><li>Piezo</li><li>Pirani</li><li>CDG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum pressure sensors market features three main types: piezoelectric, Pirani, and capacitance diaphragm gauges (CDG). Piezoelectric sensors utilize the piezoelectric effect to measure pressure changes, ideal for dynamic applications. Pirani sensors operate on thermal conductivity principles, making them suitable for low to medium vacuum ranges. Capacitance diaphragm gauges measure pressure via diaphragm displacement, offering high accuracy in varying vacuum conditions. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and research, catering to diverse vacuum measurement needs.</p></p>
