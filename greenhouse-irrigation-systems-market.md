<p><p>The greenhouse irrigation systems market encompasses various types designed to optimize water usage. Micro sprinkler irrigation uses small, low-pressure sprinklers for even distribution, ideal for sensitive crops. Sprinkler irrigation sprays water over larger areas, providing extensive coverage and flexibility. Additionally, other irrigation methods may include sub-surface irrigation or hydroponics, catering to diverse agricultural needs and enhancing crop yields while conserving water resources.</p></p>
