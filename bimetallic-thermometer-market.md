<p><p>The bimetallic thermometer market comprises three main types: angle type, straight type, and adjustable angle type. Angle type thermometers feature a dial positioned at a right angle to the sensing element, making them suitable for limited space applications. Straight type thermometers have a linear design, allowing easy installation in pipes and tanks. Adjustable angle type thermometers combine the benefits of both, enabling the user to set the dial at various angles for optimal readability in different setups.</p></p>
