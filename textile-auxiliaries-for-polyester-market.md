<p><p>Textile auxiliaries for the polyester market include various functional agents that enhance fabric processing and performance. Thickeners modify viscosity for better application of dyes. Binding agents ensure proper adhesion of pigments or fibers. Cross-linking agents improve durability and resistance to wear. Emulsifiers aid in mixing water and oil-based components. Fixing agents enhance color retention during washing. Dispersing agents promote uniform dye distribution, while defoamers reduce surface foam during processing. Other agents may include softeners or stain repellents, enhancing overall textile quality.</p></p>
