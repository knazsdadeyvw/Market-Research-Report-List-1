<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Water Desalination Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse osmosis (RO)</li><li>Multi-stage flash distillation (MSF)</li><li>Multiple-effect distillation (MED)</li><li>Hybrid</li><li>Electrodialysis (ED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water desalination plants utilize various technologies to convert seawater or brackish water into potable water. Key market types include reverse osmosis (RO), which employs a semi-permeable membrane to separate salt; multi-stage flash distillation (MSF), which uses heat to evaporate water in multiple stages; and multiple-effect distillation (MED), which enhances efficiency through cascading evaporation. Hybrid systems combine different methods for improved performance, while electrodialysis (ED) uses electric currents to separate ions, making these technologies essential for addressing global water scarcity.</p></p>
