<p><p>Agriculture adjuvants enhance the efficacy of pesticides and herbicides. Activator adjuvants improve the performance of active ingredients by increasing their absorption, spreading, or penetration, leading to improved pest control. Utility adjuvants serve functional purposes, such as reducing surface tension, stabilizing formulations, or facilitating mixing. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing agricultural practices, ensuring better crop protection, and increasing overall yield, while also helping to minimize chemical usage and environmental impact.</p></p>
