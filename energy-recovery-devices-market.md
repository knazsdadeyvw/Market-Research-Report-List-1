<p><p>Energy Recovery Devices (ERDs) optimize energy use in various applications. Reverse Turbines convert excess pressure from fluid flows into usable energy, often in water systems. Pelton Wheels harness kinetic energy from high-velocity water jets, efficiently generating power in hydroelectric plants. Pressure Exchangers transfer energy between fluid streams, maintaining efficiency in processes. Work Exchangers capture and reutilize energy from various processes. Electric Motor Drives enable energy recovery by converting mechanical energy back into electrical energy, enhancing overall system efficiency.</p></p>
