<p><p>The polyurethane foam blowing agents market consists of several types, including hydrocarbons (HC), hydrofluorocarbons (HFC), hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFC), and hydrofluorocarbons (HFO). Hydrocarbons are cost-effective and environmentally friendly but flammable. HFCs are used for their low toxicity but have high global warming potential. HCFCs are transitioning out due to their ozone-depleting properties. HFOs offer low global warming potential and improved environmental profiles, making them increasingly popular in a market focused on sustainability and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
