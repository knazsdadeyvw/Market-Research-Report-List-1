<p><strong>The Coal Gasifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Pressure Gasification</li><li>Pressurized Gasification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coal gasifier market is primarily segmented into atmospheric pressure gasification and pressurized gasification. Atmospheric pressure gasification operates at ambient pressure, focusing on low-cost processes for coal-to-gas conversion. It is suitable for small-scale applications and simpler operations. In contrast, pressurized gasification operates under elevated pressures, enhancing efficiency and gas quality, making it ideal for large-scale projects. This technology is often used to produce syngas for energy and chemical feedstock, offering higher output and better carbon capture potential.</p></p>
