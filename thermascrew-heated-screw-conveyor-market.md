<p><ul><li>Electric Heating</li><li>Media Thermal Regulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermascrew Heated Screw Conveyor market consists of two main types: electric heating and media thermal regulation. Electric heating utilizes resistance elements or heated wires integrated into the screw conveyor, providing consistent thermal control for materials requiring specific temperature management. Media thermal regulation, on the other hand, employs external heating mediums, such as hot water or oil, circulating around the conveyor for uniform temperature maintenance. Both systems enhance process efficiency, ensuring optimal material handling and preventing issues related to temperature-sensitive substances.</p></p>
