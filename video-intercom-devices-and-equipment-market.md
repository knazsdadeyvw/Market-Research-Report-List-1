<p><p>Video intercom devices and equipment are categorized into two main types: Analog and IP. Analog video intercoms transmit signals over traditional coaxial cables, offering simplicity and reliability, often in smaller installations. In contrast, IP video intercoms leverage internet protocol networks, allowing for advanced features like remote access, higher video quality, and integration with smart home systems. The IP type typically caters to larger, more complex setups, providing scalability and enhanced functionality compared to its analog counterpart.</p></p>
