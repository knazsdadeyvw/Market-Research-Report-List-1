<p><p>The Cleanroom Pass Box market is segmented into two main types: Static Pass Box and Dynamic Pass Box. Static Pass Boxes are designed for secure transfer of materials without air circulation, minimizing contamination risk in controlled environments. Conversely, Dynamic Pass Boxes feature automated mechanisms that allow for simultaneous entry and exit of items, maintaining airflow control and reducing personnel exposure. Both types serve crucial roles in industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics, ensuring compliance with strict cleanliness standards.</p></p>
