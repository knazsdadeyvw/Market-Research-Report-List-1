<p><p>The radiofrequency coil market comprises various types, primarily categorized into volume coils and surface coils. Both types play crucial roles in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), enhancing diagnostic capabilities and improving patient outcomes through tailored imaging solutions.</p></p>
