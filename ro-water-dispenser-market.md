<p><strong>The RO Water Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Water Dispenser</li><li>POE RO Water Dispenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO water dispenser market can be categorized into two main types: Point of Use (POU) RO water dispensers and Point of Entry (POE) RO water dispensers. POU RO dispensers are designed for individual use, providing purified water directly from a tap or a dedicated dispenser at the consumption point, ideal for homes and offices. In contrast, POE RO dispensers treat water at the entry point of the building, ensuring that all water used within the premises is purified, making them suitable for larger facilities.</p></p>
