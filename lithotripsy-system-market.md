<p><p>The lithotripsy system market is primarily categorized into three types: Shock Wave Lithotripsy (SWL), Laser Lithotripsy, and Other methods. SWL utilizes high-energy shock waves to break kidney stones into smaller pieces, making them easier to pass. Laser Lithotripsy employs focused laser energy to fragment stones with precision, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. The "Other" category includes alternative techniques such as ultrasound lithotripsy and endoscopic methods, which offer additional options based on specific patient needs and stone characteristics.</p></p>
