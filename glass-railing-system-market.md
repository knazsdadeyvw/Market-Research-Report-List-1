<p><p>The Glass Railing System market includes two primary types: frameless and framed glass railings. Frameless glass railings feature continuous glass panels secured with minimal hardware, offering a sleek, unobstructed view and a modern aesthetic, making them popular in residential and commercial applications. In contrast, framed glass railings use metal or structural frameworks to support glass panels, providing enhanced stability and a traditional look. Both options are favored for safety, durability, and their ability to complement various architectural styles.</p></p>
