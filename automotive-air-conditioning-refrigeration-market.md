Single Zone Automatic AC regulates temperature in one area of the vehicle, providing consistent comfort for all passengers. In contrast, Multizone Automatic AC allows for individualized climate control across different sections, enabling passengers to adjust temperature and airflow preferences according to their needs. This diversity enhances passenger comfort and satisfaction, catering to varying climate preferences within the vehicle.</p></p>
