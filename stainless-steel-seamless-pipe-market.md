<p><p>The stainless steel seamless pipe market is primarily divided into two categories: cold drawn and hot rolled. Cold drawn pipes are produced by drawing the material through a die at room temperature, resulting in tighter tolerances, improved surface finish, and enhanced mechanical properties, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, hot rolled pipes are manufactured by heating the steel above its recrystallization temperature, allowing for easier shaping and larger diameters but often with lower dimensional precision.</p></p>
