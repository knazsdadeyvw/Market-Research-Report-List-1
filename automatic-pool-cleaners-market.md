<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure Type</li><li>Positive Pressure Type</li><li>Robotic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaners market is divided into three key types: </p><p>1. **Negative Pressure Type**: These cleaners use suction to draw debris into a bag or the pool's filtration system, offering effective cleaning but may require a stronger pool pump.</p><p>2. **Positive Pressure Type**: These models use water pressure to propel themselves and push debris into a collection bag, requiring a booster pump for optimal performance.</p><p>3. **Robotic Type**: Autonomous, programmable devices that utilize electric power and intelligent navigation, providing thorough cleaning with minimal manual intervention.</p></p>
