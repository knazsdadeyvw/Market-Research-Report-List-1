<p><strong>The Image Guided Surgery Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)</li><li>X-Ray Fluoroscopy</li><li>Endoscopes</li><li>Position Emission Tomography (PET)</li><li>Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Image Guided Surgery Devices encompass various imaging modalities that enhance surgical precision. Computed Tomography (CT) offers cross-sectional images for detailed visualization. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides superior soft tissue contrast. X-Ray Fluoroscopy delivers real-time imaging during procedures. Endoscopes allow direct visualization within the body. Position Emission Tomography (PET) enables functional imaging, while Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) assesses metabolic processes. These technologies collectively improve surgical outcomes by guiding interventions, minimizing invasiveness, and increasing accuracy in targeting affected areas.</p></p>
