<p><p>The HVDC valves market consists of two primary types: conventional DC valves and flexible DC valves. Conventional DC valves are used in traditional HVDC systems, providing reliable power transmission over long distances with fixed operational characteristics. In contrast, flexible DC valves offer enhanced control and adaptability, enabling dynamic power flow management and integration of renewable energy sources. This flexibility is crucial for modern energy networks, which require more responsive systems to accommodate varying supply and demand conditions.</p></p>
